Welcome to KnightRook, a real-time multiplayer chess platform engineered for performance, precision, and aesthetics. 

KnightRook combines modern web technologies with deep analytical engines to provide an uncompromising chess experience. Whether you are a casual player or a grandmaster, the platform adapts to your pace and style.

### Features

**Real-Time Multiplayer Integration**  
Create a private room, share the room code with your friends, and play instantly. Every move, clock tick, and interaction is synchronized globally with zero latency.

**Premium Minimalist Interface**  
The interface is designed to be immersive and frictionless. Featuring a sleek, dark-mode aesthetic with striking gold accents, the UI ensures your total focus remains exactly where it belongs: on the board.

**The Gauntlet: Solo Opponents**  
You think you’re ready for the big leagues? Meet the board. 
- **Muffin:** The rookie. He’s cute, he’s predictable, and he’s here to make you feel good about yourself. Beat him, and maybe you get to keep your job.
- **Ace:** The closer. He doesn’t play games; he ends them. He’s going to read your blunders before you even touch the piece. If you’re not bringing your A-game, don’t even bother sitting at the table.
- **Valentino:** The managing partner. He’s cold, he’s calculating, and he doesn’t have time for your excuses. He’s going to dismantle your position, take your pieces, and make you thank him for the privilege. Play him when you want to know what losing to the best actually feels like.

**Psychological Warfare**  
Chess is a mental game. KnightRook includes an integrated taunt system, allowing you to deploy perfectly timed feline taunts against your opponent when you find a winning tactic.
